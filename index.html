Graphing Pollution Levels of Chinese Air

Reports of high levels of air pollution in China are nothing new, but recent reports have startled many. These are reports of air quality levels so low that even the usually docile citizenry has begun to protest.

The United States Embassy runs a twitter account which tweets the current levels of air pollution in Beijing. The values tweeted are points on the Air Quality Index. Apparently the authorities have threatened to shut it down multiple times but not yet followed through.

I think it's a great idea and I've decided to graph the data, since it's rather tough to digest in tweet form. This graph will update hourly, just as the twitter feed does.
